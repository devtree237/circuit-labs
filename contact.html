Frequently Asked Questions

How do I set up an account?

To set up an account, visit the "Sign Up" page and fill out the registration form. You'll need to provide your name, email address, and create a password. Once your account is created, you can log in and start exploring the circuit-building game.

What resources are available?

We have a variety of resources available, including tutorial videos, guides, and a knowledge base. You can find these by visiting the "Resources" page on our website. If you don't see what you're looking for, feel free to reach out to our support team.

How do I troubleshoot issues?

If you're experiencing any issues with the game or your account, please start by checking our FAQ section and troubleshooting guide. If you can't find a solution, you can contact our support team, who will be happy to assist you.
